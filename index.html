MINING BITCOIN IN 2019

The age of Bitcoin has already crossed the ten-year mark. In the beginning, almost no one knew about him, but the whole world has learned the last few years. Even large news portals are actively interested in cryptocurrency and show the course of Bitcoin on the main page.
